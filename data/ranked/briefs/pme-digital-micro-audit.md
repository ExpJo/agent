## Product definition — Week 1 MVP

20-question digital readiness diagnostic for Montreal SMBs below ESSOR's $2.5M revenue threshold. Produces a prioritized action PDF with PME MTL resource links.

**Ship in 7 days:**
- Industry-tuned questionnaire (FR primary)
- Priority matrix output (effort vs impact)
- PDF report with top 3 recommended investments
- PME MTL centre links embedded
- Stripe: $199 self-serve; $499 with 1hr Cal.com review

**NOT in v1:** ESSOR application filing, ERP implementation, on-site visits.
