## Product definition — Week 1 MVP

Downloadable French workplace onboarding materials tailored to Montreal restaurant roles — phrase cards for kitchen, service, and bar positions, plus a manager progress tracker for TFW staff francization.

**Ship in 7 days:**
- Role-based FR phrase cards (PDF): server, line cook, dishwasher, host
- Onboarding checklist (Day 1 / Week 1 / Month 1)
- Simple progress tracker (web form → dashboard)
- Stripe: $79 kit + optional $29/mo tracker
- FR/EN manager UI

**NOT in v1:** Francisation Québec integration, certified translation, HRIS sync.
