## Product definition — Week 1 MVP

Self-serve digital workbook for Montreal commercial tenants facing lease renewal — lease key-date extraction, negotiation script templates (FR), and stay/leave decision matrix.

**Ship in 7 days:**
- Manual lease data entry form (key dates, current rent, proposed rent)
- Negotiation script templates in French
- Decision matrix PDF export
- Email course drip (5 emails over 2 weeks)
- Stripe: $49 workbook; $149 with 30-min review call

**NOT in v1:** Legal review, automated comp database, lease OCR.
