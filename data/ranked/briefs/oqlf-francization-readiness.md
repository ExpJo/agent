**Rank:** #5 | **MOVS:** 8.0 | **Status:** hypothesis  
**Category:** compliance-regtech | **Build:** 7 days

---

## Product definition — Week 1 MVP

Self-assessment questionnaire for Montreal employers approaching the 25-employee Bill 96 francization threshold. Outputs a gap score dashboard and prioritized action checklist PDF.

**Ship in 7 days:**
- FR/EN questionnaire (~30 questions mapped to OQLF requirements)
- Gap score dashboard (visual red/yellow/green)
- PDF export with prioritized action items
- Stripe Checkout: $149 self-serve, $299 with email support
- Results emailed to HR contact

**NOT in v1:** OQLF filing integration, legal advice, employee LMS.
